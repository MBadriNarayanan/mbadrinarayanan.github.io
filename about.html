About Me

I am pursuing my Master in Computer Sciences at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. My most recent work experience was at Nokia Bell Labs as an Autonomous System Research Intern during Summer 2024 where I worked with LLMs in the context of automation and collaborative agents.
At UW-Madison, I worked with Dr. Suman Banerjee to benchmark and deploy LLMs on resource-constrained devices. Before this, I worked as an Associate Engineer - AI/ML at Qualcomm, Hyderabad as part of the AISW team.
I completed my bachelor's in Information Technology at Sri Sivasubramaniya Nadar College of Engineering, Chennai. My primary interests lie in the fields of Machine Learning (ML), Natural Language Processing (NLP), Large Language Models (LLMs), Computer Vision (CV), and Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR).
With a strong academic background, industry exposure, and a passion for pushing the boundaries of AI, I am well-equipped to contribute to cutting-edge research and development efforts, ultimately translating theoretical concepts into practical and innovative solutions that address real-world challenges.
