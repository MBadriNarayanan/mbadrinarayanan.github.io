About Me

I am currently working at CuroNow as a Founding AI / ML Engineer, where I build intelligent agentic systems that enhance the platform's AI capabilities.
I am a Master's graduate in Computer Science from the University of Wisconsin-Madison, where I developed an end-to-end Agentic-RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) pipeline for radiology reports. This pipeline significantly improved our ability to extract and analyze insights from healthcare data.
My most recent work experience was at Nokia Bell Labs as an Autonomous Systems Research Intern during the summer of 2024, where I worked with LLMs in the context of automation and collaborative agents.
At UW-Madison, I worked with Dr. Suman Banerjee to benchmark and deploy LLMs on resource-constrained devices and our work was presented in ICLR 2025. Prior to this, I worked as an Associate Engineer - AI/ML at Qualcomm, Hyderabad, as part of the AISW team. My role involved running inference for state-of-the-art ML models and improving the in-house testing pipeline.
I completed my bachelor's degree in Information Technology at Sri Sivasubramaniya Nadar College of Engineering, Chennai. My primary interests lie in the fields of Generative AI (GenAI), Large Language Models (LLMs), Agentic Systems, Machine Learning (ML), Natural Language Processing (NLP).
With a strong academic background, industry exposure, and a passion for advancing AI, I am well-equipped to contribute to cutting-edge research and development efforts. I am driven by the goal of translating theoretical concepts into practical and innovative solutions that address real-world challenges.
